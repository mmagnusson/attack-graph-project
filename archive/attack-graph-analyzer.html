ATT&CK Graph Analyzer — Usage Guide

Purpose: This tool visualizes the MITRE ATT&CK framework as an interactive graph to help you determine where limited defensive budget is best spent. It answers: "Which technique, if I invest in detecting it, breaks the most attack paths used by my threat actors?"

Click nodes to toggle detection coverage on/off. Green = covered, red = uncovered.

Select threat actors in the left panel to overlay their known TTPs on the graph. Selected actor techniques glow pink.

Recompute Priorities runs centrality analysis across all attack paths formed by selected threat actors, weighted by your current coverage gaps. The top recommendations are techniques where detection investment would disrupt the most attack chains.

Layouts: Force (organic clustering), Radial (tactic-centric), Hierarchy (kill chain flow).

Zoom: Scroll to zoom, drag to pan. Ctrl+click to multi-select.

Search: Filter by technique ID or name. Click tactic labels to show/hide entire tactic groups.

Data: Representative ATT&CK subset (Enterprise). For production use, load full STIX bundle via ATT&CK's TAXII server.
